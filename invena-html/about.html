About Us About Us

Ifnoss - Student Information Management System

00+

Year of experience

IFNOSS is an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system which integrates all data and processes of an educational institution into a unified platform. It is a comprehensive Education Information Management Software designed to streamline academic, administrative and operational activities of educational institutions.

The administration of educational institutions is a demanding and complex task involving the collection, storage and sharing of large volumes of data. Manual handling of this data is time-consuming, error-prone and inefficient. IFNOSS eliminates these challenges by providing a centralized, automated and reliable system.

About Us More About IFNOSS

A Complete ERP & Education Information Management System for Universities, Colleges, Schools and Institutes

Developed in close coordination with educationists, IFNOSS is an all-encompassing school and institution management system that connects all departments and processes on a single integrated platform, making data management, retrieval and updates simple and efficient.
